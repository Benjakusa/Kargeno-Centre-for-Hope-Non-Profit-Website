Volunteer with Us

Your time and skills can change lives

Volunteering with Kar Geno is a powerful way to give back and make a tangible impact. Whether you’re a professional with expertise to offer, or someone passionate about helping others, we welcome individuals who want to contribute to the transformative work we’re doing in Western Kenya.

Your time and skills can change lives. At Kar Geno, we are always looking for dedicated individuals who are eager to work on our community projects. Whether you have specific skills or simply a strong desire to help, there is a place for you in our mission. Your support will directly empower women, children, and families in the region, helping them rebuild their lives and create lasting change.

Visit & Experience the Community

Immerse yourself in our vibrant community

Understanding a community’s needs is best done by immersing yourself in it. We invite you to visit Kar Geno and see our projects firsthand, even if you’re not able to volunteer. Come interact with the women, children, and young people of Kar Geno and experience the rich, traditional African culture. Share in local meals, learn about the challenges they face, and discover how you can contribute to their growth and transformation.

Internships for Students & Professionals

Gain hands-on experience in community development

Kar Geno offers internship opportunities for students and professionals looking to gain hands-on experience in community development. If you’re passionate about empowering marginalized communities, an internship with us provides the chance to make a real impact while gaining valuable skills. Work alongside our dedicated team, and immerse yourself in a unique learning experience in Western Kenya.

If your educational institution or employer requires official documentation for your internship, we are happy to provide an official letter acknowledging your placement and work with us.

Join Us in Changing Lives

Be a part of the change you want to see

Your involvement—whether through volunteering, visiting, or interning—helps us continue our vital work and supports our mission to empower communities and restore hope. Come, experience, and be a part of the change you want to see in the world.

Your Donation Helps Empower

Every contribution makes a difference

At Kar Geno, we believe that hope is a powerful catalyst for change. The HIV/AIDS epidemic has had a profound impact on the communities of Western Kenya, leaving many families struggling to recover. Women, in particular, are often left to shoulder the burdens of the crisis, caring for their children and trying to rebuild their lives. With your support, we can provide resources they need to rebuild their lives, regain their dignity and create a better future.

$10 Provides a reusable sanitary towel for a school-going girl, ensuring she has the dignity and comfort to attend school without interruption.
$15 Provides a food parcel to sustain a family for a week, offering much-needed nourishment to help families through challenging times.
$20 Provides women with lunch for a week as they undertake their tailoring course, helping them stay energized and focused while learning valuable skills.
$25 Helps provide program training materials for a week, giving women the resources they need to succeed and build a future of self-reliance.
$30 Provides school learning materials for school-going children for one academic term, ensuring they have the tools to succeed in their education.
$50 Covers two months of a women’s tailoring course, equipping her with skills to start a sustainable business and become financially independent.
$100 Empowers a woman to start her own business, giving her the foundation to build a self-reliant future and contribute to the community’s growth.
$150 Helps provide women with the necessary farm tools for irrigation, ensuring sustainable agriculture throughout the year.
$500 Provides tuition for school-going children for one year, ensuring they have the opportunity to receive a quality education.

Reach out to join our mission

Kar Geno is located in Manyatta, nestled between Obinju Primary School and Manyatta Market in Kisumu City, Western Kenya. Our community-based organization is easily accessible, with a seven-hour bus ride or one hour flight from Nairobi, the capital city of Kenya.

Kisumu is Kenya’s third-largest city, known for its rich cultural heritage, vibrant communities, and beautiful location along the shores of Lake Victoria. The city is a hub for economic and social activity in the region, making it an ideal place for our mission of empowerment and community development.
